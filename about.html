About Us

Jedot Holding Services is a creative and service-driven brand founded by a mother and daughter duo — Dorothy and Jessica — who share a passion for both artistic expression and meaningful service. The name Jedot blends the first two letters of Jessica (Je) and Dorothy (Dot), symbolizing unity, creativity, and family.

Both founders are naturally artistic, expressing their creativity through fashion design, music, and writing. Professionally, Dorothy is a certified accountant and assistant nurse, while Jessica brings energy and originality through artistic pursuits and educational support.

Their services include:

  • Styling and creation of African designer clothing
  • Songwriting and music production with depth and purpose
  • Creative writing, including books inspired by life experience
  • Accounting and financial literacy support
  • Nursing assistance and basic English tutoring

At Jedot Holding Services, artistry meets compassion and professionalism — blending skills, creativity, and care to serve with impact.
