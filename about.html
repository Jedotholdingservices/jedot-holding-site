About Us

Jedot Holding Services is a brand born from the shared passions, professions, and creativity of a mother and daughter duo — Dorothy and Jessica. The name Jedot combines the first two letters of Jessica (Je) and Dorothy (Dot), representing the unity of their talents and vision.

Rooted in artistic expression and professional expertise, Jedot offers a unique blend of services. Dorothy brings her experience as a certified accountant and assistant nurse, while Jessica contributes her creative gifts in music, fashion, and writing.

Their offerings include:

  • Styling and design of authentic African attire
  • Songwriting and music production with depth and purpose
  • Book writing drawn from instinct, experience, and personal growth
  • Support services in accounting, finance, and basic English tutoring
  • Compassionate nursing assistance

At Jedot, creativity meets purpose — combining artistry, care, and knowledge to serve with impact.
