Bothersome Kate

By Dorothy Ifeanyi Makasi

Bothersome Kate is a humorous and heartwarming story about a Nigerian girl with a big mouth, a bold spirit, and a knack for turning every moment into a memorable one.

At school, she’s the self-appointed class reporter. At home, she questions every adult decision. And in church? Let’s just say even the pastor isn’t safe from her blunt observations.

Written with wit and warmth, Bothersome Kate is a celebration of bold Black girl energy, cultural pride, and the hilarious chaos of childhood.

The Skinflint

By Dorothy Ifeanyi Makasi

Mr. Odiegwu is a man of ambition, charm, and complexity. In a society riddled with inequality, his cunning survival instincts drive him through a series of morally ambiguous ventures. As he chases wealth and status by any means necessary, he navigates deception, manipulation, and unexpected consequences. "The Skinflint" is a thought-provoking tale of ambition and morality, exposing the cost of success when conscience is set aside. Captivating and bold, this novel explores the thin line between desperation and determination.
